About Me

I am Jessenia Kahn, a full-stack software engineer based out of Atlanta. I have many passions, but technology rules them all. As a young child, I would disassemble/re-assemble any electronic device I could get my hands on. I received my first computer from a family friend at the age of 9 and spent most of my adolesence behind some console. In high school, I took my first course in programming but spent most of the time competing with other students in a high stakes custom retro-tetris game ($5 lunch money was a lot back then). As of a matter of fact, I did not become serious about taking up technology as a career until my sophomore year in college. I debated between computer science, finance, or accounting after realizing my focus in music was just not going to pan out. Did I mention I was a highly decorated trumpet player in high school? Lucky for me, the school chose for me. I wanted to do financing so I applied and I did not get in, so the school put me in Management Information Systems. Wait a minute...
